Q3. Create a table and explain how to acheive colspan

Colspan is used to combine two columns together

        <table border="">
            <tr>
                <td colspan="2">Name</td>
                <td>Age</td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
                <td>Dipendra</td>

            </tr>
